Getting akanname

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Days And Names

Male Names

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwaku
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friady: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female Names

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friady: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
